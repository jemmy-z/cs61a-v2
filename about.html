About

Bio

I am going to be a third year Computer Science major in the fall semester. I grew up in the Bay Area (Fremont!), so I've pretty much lived here my whole life. I took my first CS class in senior year of high school and it gradually grew on me. I thought I was pretty decent at the time, but then in CS 61A, I realized there was A LOT I didn't know. While CS 61A is definitely a challenging class, the rewards at the end definitely make the journey worth it. And I'm here to help!

Right after I took the course, I become an Academic Intern in Spring 2018 and started learning the ropes of how to effectively teach students the material. In Summer 2018, I became a Tutor for the course and started leading small group sections to further enhance their understanding of the material. This summer will be my first time teaching larger sections as a TA, and my goal is for everyone to have a better understanding of the fundamentals of CS.

Tips for Success
  • Start early
  • Ask for help
  • Check out Piazza for more help
  • Get a lot of practice
  • Review your mistakes and learn from them
  • Come to section!
